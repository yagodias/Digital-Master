Dark Matter

Dark matter, like normal matter (made up of protons, neutrons and electrons), has gravity, exerting a force of attraction on matter. It is called dark because it does not emit electromagnetic radiation, and therefore cannot be detected in any range of the electromagnetic spectrum.
